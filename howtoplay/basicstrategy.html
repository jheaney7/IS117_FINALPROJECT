Basic Strategy

Understanding when to bet, fold, and bluff is critical in poker. Here are some beginner strategies:

  • Position: Play more hands when you're in a later position at the table.
  • Starting Hands: Only play strong hands in early positions.
  • Bluffing: Use bluffs sparingly and only when the situation is right.
