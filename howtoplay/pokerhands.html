Poker Hands and Rankings

  • Royal Flush: A, K, Q, J, 10 of the same suit. Best hand possible.  
  • Straight Flush: Five consecutive cards of the same suit.  
  • Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same rank.  
  • Full House: Three of a kind and a pair.  
  • Flush: Any five cards of the same suit.  
  • Straight: Three of a kind and a pair.  
  • Three of a Kind: Any five cards of the same suit.  
  • Two Pairs: Three of a kind and a pair.  
  • Pair: Any five cards of the same suit.  
  • High Card: Highest random five cards not falling in any other category.
